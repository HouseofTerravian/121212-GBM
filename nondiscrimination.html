Non-Discrimination Policy

A marketplace built by us — open to all

Our Position

121212 GBM — The Global Black Marketplace — exists to amplify Black enterprise and build generational wealth across the global diaspora. At the same time, this platform is for ALL PEOPLE. Every buyer, supporter, and ally is welcome here.

"Black" on this platform is defined as one self-identifying as Black, whatever that means to them.

Per the 121212.black home website, our official definition of Black is: "one who goes within, within the triplefold darkness, the 121212, the void, and knows their Creator, or, sees the light within."

This definition is intentionally expansive. It is spiritual, not exclusionary. It speaks to an inner journey — not a gatekeeping mechanism. The 121212 movement embraces all who resonate with this vision.

Non-Discrimination Commitment

121212 GBM does not discriminate against any user — buyer, vendor, or visitor — on the basis of:

  • Race, color, or ethnicity
  • National origin or ancestry
  • Gender, gender identity, or gender expression
  • Sexual orientation
  • Religion or spiritual belief
  • Age
  • Disability (physical, mental, or sensory)
  • Veteran or military status
  • Socioeconomic status
  • Any other characteristic protected by applicable law

Vendor Self-Identification

Vendors on 121212 GBM self-identify as Black or as part of the African diaspora. This process is:

  • Voluntary: No one is compelled to disclose personal identity details beyond what they choose to share.
  • Trust-Based: We do not require documentation, genetic testing, or proof of ancestry. We trust our community.
  • Community-Accountable: Our verification process is rooted in mutual respect and good faith, not surveillance.

Buyer Access

Anyone may shop on 121212 GBM. There are no identity requirements for buyers. We welcome all people who wish to support Black-owned businesses and the global diaspora economy.

Reporting Discrimination

If you experience or witness discrimination on the 121212 GBM platform, we encourage you to report it immediately. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
