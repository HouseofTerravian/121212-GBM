Our Story

Built by the diaspora.
For the diaspora.

The Global Black Marketplace exists to build generational wealth across the global Black community — one product, one purchase, one vendor at a time.

The Origin

Why we built this.

For centuries, Black entrepreneurs across the globe have created extraordinary things — handcrafted goods, art, food, fashion, wellness — and yet the systems designed to help them scale were never designed with them in mind.

The Global Black Marketplace — 121212 GBM — is our answer to that gap. A dedicated, community-first digital marketplace where Black-owned businesses can reach a global audience, keep more of what they earn, and build something that lasts.

"Every purchase on GBM is an investment in someone's legacy. That's not a slogan — it's the architecture."

We launched because we believe the Black dollar is one of the most powerful economic forces on Earth — and it has been historically redirected everywhere except back into Black communities. GBM is infrastructure for recirculation.

Our platform connects buyers who want to shop with intention to verified Black vendors across 75+ countries — from Ghana to Jamaica, Angola to the UK, Brazil to the USA. The diaspora is enormous. We're making it connective.

How We Operate

Our guiding principles.

01

Identity is Voluntary

Vendors self-identify as Black-owned. We do not impose a definition. We verify through a trust-based process and community accountability — not surveillance.

02

Economics Over Optics

GBM is not a feel-good project. It is economic infrastructure. Every decision — pricing, vendor tools, discovery — is made through the lens of wealth creation.

03

Diaspora Includes Everyone

Black in Ghana. Black in Brazil. Black in Paris. Black in Detroit. We don't rank or sort Blackness. Every corner of the global community is equally welcome here.

04

Sellers Are Partners

Vendors are not users — they are partners. Their success is our success. Platform decisions are made with seller impact at the center, not as an afterthought.

05

Buyers Are Community

People who shop on GBM are not just customers — they are investors in Black enterprise. We build tools and experiences that honor that intentionality.

06

Long Game Always

We are not building for an exit. We are building for legacy. Every feature, every policy, every relationship is designed to compound over generations — not quarters.
