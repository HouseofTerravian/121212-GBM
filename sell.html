Common Questions

Do I need to be verified to sell?

No documentation is required. GBM operates on self-identification. We trust our community members to represent themselves honestly.

When do I receive payment?

Payments are processed after order confirmation. Funds are typically available within 3–5 business days, depending on your payment method.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle. Your storefront and listings remain active during any transition.

What does "platform fee" mean?

GBM takes a small percentage of each sale to cover payment processing and platform costs. The remainder goes directly to you.

Can I sell digital products?

Yes. GBM supports physical goods, digital downloads, and services including courses, coaching, and music.

What is the GBM Verified badge?

The Verified badge signals to buyers that your storefront has been reviewed and meets GBM community standards. All plan tiers are eligible.
