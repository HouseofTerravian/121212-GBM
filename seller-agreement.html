Seller Agreement

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Overview

This Seller Agreement ("Agreement") governs your participation as a vendor on the 121212 GBM platform ("The Global Black Marketplace"), operated by MCM Enterprises, a DBA of SlateRiver LLC, a New Mexico LLC. By creating a vendor account, you agree to the terms outlined in this Agreement.

2. Eligibility

To sell on 121212 GBM, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age (or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction).
  • Self-identify as Black or as part of the African diaspora. This is a trust-based, voluntary process — no documentation is required.
  • Have the legal right to sell the products or services you list.
  • Agree to abide by all applicable laws in your jurisdiction regarding commerce, taxation, and consumer protection.

3. Vendor Plans & Fees

121212 GBM offers three vendor tiers:

  • Griot (Free): Up to 10 product listings. Platform fee: 10% per completed sale.
  • Merchant ($49/month): Up to 100 product listings. Platform fee: 5% per completed sale. Includes custom storefront, marketing tools, analytics, and priority support.
  • Mogul ($200/month): Unlimited product listings. Platform fee: 2% per completed sale. Includes premium storefront, custom domain, dedicated account manager, homepage features, and early access to new features.

Platform fees are deducted automatically from each sale before disbursement. Additional payment processing fees from third-party providers may apply.

4. Vendor Obligations

As a vendor, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate, truthful product descriptions, pricing, and images.
  • Fulfill orders in a timely manner and communicate shipping timelines to buyers.
  • Provide responsive customer service and handle buyer inquiries professionally.
  • Comply with all applicable shipping regulations, customs requirements, and import/export laws.
  • Not list prohibited items including but not limited to: illegal goods, counterfeit products, weapons, hazardous materials, or any items that violate third-party intellectual property rights.
  • Maintain the quality standards expected by the 121212 GBM community.

5. Product Listings

Vendors retain ownership of their product content (descriptions, images, and media) that they upload to the platform. By listing products on 121212 GBM, you grant the platform a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to display, promote, and distribute your product content within the marketplace and its marketing channels.

121212 GBM reserves the right to remove any listing that violates this Agreement, our Terms & Conditions, or community standards — with or without prior notice.

6. Payments & Disbursements

  • Payments from buyers are processed through secure third-party payment providers.
  • Vendor earnings (sale amount minus platform fee and payment processing fees) are disbursed according to the payment schedule established with your selected payment provider.
  • Typical disbursement timeline: 3–5 business days after order confirmation.
  • Vendors are solely responsible for reporting income and paying applicable taxes in their jurisdiction.

7. Refunds & Returns

Vendors are required to establish and clearly communicate their own refund and return policies to buyers. At minimum, vendors must:

  • Respond to refund requests within 5 business days.
  • Honor refunds for items that arrive damaged, defective, or significantly not as described.
  • Process approved refunds within 10 business days.

121212 GBM may intervene in disputes between buyers and vendors when necessary to protect the integrity of the marketplace.

8. GBM Verified Badge

Vendors on all plan tiers are eligible for the GBM Verified badge. Verification is based on community trust, storefront completeness, and adherence to platform standards. The badge may be revoked if a vendor violates this Agreement or community guidelines.

9. Platform Intellectual Property

All intellectual property rights in and to the 121212 GBM platform — including the website, branding, logos, software, and proprietary content — are owned exclusively by Chude Muonelo. Vendors may not use 121212 GBM branding, logos, or trademarks without prior written permission.

10. Account Suspension & Termination

121212 GBM reserves the right to suspend or terminate vendor accounts for:

  • Violation of this Agreement or the Terms & Conditions.
  • Repeated buyer complaints or unresolved disputes.
  • Listing prohibited or counterfeit products.
  • Fraudulent activity or misrepresentation.
  • Extended inactivity (180+ days with no listings or sales).

Vendors may voluntarily close their account at any time. Outstanding earnings will be disbursed according to the standard payment schedule.

11. Indemnification

Vendors agree to indemnify and hold harmless 121212 GBM, MCM Enterprises, SlateRiver LLC, Chude Muonelo, and their respective officers, agents, and employees from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from the vendor's products, services, content, or breach of this Agreement.

12. Modifications

121212 GBM may modify this Seller Agreement at any time. Vendors will be notified of material changes. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Agreement.
